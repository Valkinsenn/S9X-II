We are Squad 9

Squad 9 is a worldwide community of players that formed on Halo: Combat Evolved for PC in 2003. We have maintained an active presence in the world of FPS gaming ever since. We aspire to become a top-tier gaming organization that offers our members the best team experience in today's gaming universe:
