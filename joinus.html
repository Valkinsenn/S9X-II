Welcome to Squad 9, Recruits!

Thank you for your interest in joining us.

We are currently accepting applications for membership.
Before applying however, please ensure you meet the following criteria:


  1. Be at least 16 years old.
  2. Have a working microphone or headset.
  3. Have Discord installed and ready to use.
  4. Play at least one of the games we operate on.
  5. Are not a part of any other groups or clans requiring tags.

If you meet all the above requirements, click the button below, fill out the form, and submit it to us!

You will be contacted by our Leadership over Steam or Discord if you are accepted.

Code of Conduct

Our Code of Conduct contains nine points that govern our community and counter toxicity.

The Code has been the bedrock of Squad 9's formation and continues to be the driving force behind our existence as a group. Anyone representing us in competitive venues is expected to conduct themselves according to our standards - even if their involvement is limited exclusively to playing comp, we have zero tolerance for toxic players.

Tags Agreement

Members of ~S9~ are expected to wear our tags and no others. This is to avoid conflicting loyalties and keeps our member base united. Anyone wishing to join another group must request a discharge. Squad 9 members are not allowed to have multi-clan or cross-clan membership. Please read through the above text carefully before you answer!

Non-Toxicity Agreement

Squad 9 has a zero-tolerance policy on toxic behavior.

While we keep things relatively loose and maintain a light-hearted, easygoing community, we work at all times to ensure that the general atmosphere never loses its foundation of mutual respect in all interactions. Please read through the above text carefully before you answer!

Fair Play Agreement

Since its founding, Squad 9 has always prided itself on playing fair online, both within and apart from the team.

Any member who is found to be using hacks or mods to bolster their competitive skill level; or unfairly exploiting online gameplay; or participating repeatedly in hacked lobbies or servers will receive a permanent ban.
